History

Rafting Sports was founded in 1985 by John and Jane Smith, two experienced river guides with a passion for adventure. Their goal was to share their love of whitewater rafting with others and provide a safe, exhilarating experience for everyone who joined them on the river.

Today, Rafting Sports is a leading provider of whitewater rafting adventures in the region, with a fleet of top-of-the-line rafts and a dedicated staff of guides and support personnel. John and Jane have retired, but their legacy lives on in the company they built, which continues to provide unforgettable experiences and lifelong memories for thousands of guests each year.
