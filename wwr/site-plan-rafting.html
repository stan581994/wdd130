Overview

Purpose

Our purpose is to reach a wider audience and make it easier for potential customers to find and book our services. Through our online presence, we aim to showcase the unique and exhilarating experience of rafting in our location, and provide comprehensive information on our packages, rates, and safety measures.

Additionally, we want to establish ourselves as a reliable and trustworthy provider of rafting adventures, by offering online booking and payment options, as well as transparent reviews and testimonials from satisfied customers.

Our business will also serve as a platform for sharing valuable information about the local area, including nearby accommodations, attractions, and activities that complement the rafting experience. By building a community around our brand and engaging with our customers through social media, email newsletters, and other online channels, we hope to establish long-term relationships with our customers and create a memorable and enjoyable experience for all.

Audience

Our target audience for the water rafting website is adventure-seeking individuals and groups who are interested in outdoor activities and are looking for a thrilling and memorable experience. Specifically, we are targeting individuals between the ages of 18-45 who are physically fit and enjoy outdoor activities such as hiking, camping, and water sports.

We are also targeting families with children over the age of 12, who are looking for a fun and exciting outdoor adventure that they can experience together. Additionally, we are targeting corporate groups, team building events, and school groups who are interested in team bonding activities and outdoor adventures.

Our target audience is primarily located in the surrounding areas of our rafting location, but we also aim to attract visitors from other regions and international travelers who are interested in adventure tourism. We aim to provide a safe and memorable rafting experience for all of our customers, regardless of their level of experience or skill level.
